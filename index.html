SZNZ: Spring

Released March 2022

Musicians:

  • Rivers Cuomo, Lead vocals, backing vocals, lead guitar & writer
  • Patrick Wilson, Drums & Writer
  • Brian Bell, Backing vocals & lead guitar
  • Scott Shriner, Backing vocals & bass

SZNZ is a collection of four short studio records to be released on the first day of each seasonal solstice, with each having its own motif, aesthetic themes, and production style based on the corresponding season. SZNZ: Spring is the first (of four) in the SZNZ series of EPs by Weezer.

Side One

  • Opening Night
  • Angels on Vacation
  • A Little Bit of Love
  • The Garden of Eden

Side Two

  • The Sound of Drums
  • All This Love
  • Wild at heart
  • The album was produced by Weezer's frequent collaborators Jake Sinclair and Suzy Shinn, as well as new collaborator Ethan Gruska.
  • Cuomo stated that he wanted the Spring album to feature a "Happy, chill, stress-free" theme, later expressing a desire for an "acoustic, breezy" song in a similar vein to "Island in the Sun".
  • Brian Bell, lead guitarist
  • SZNZ was officially announced via Weezer's social media pages on March 11 2022.

SZNZ: Summer

Released June 2022

Musicians:

  • Rivers Cuomo, Lead vocals, backing vocals, lead guitar & writer
  • Patrick Wilson, Drums
  • Brian Bell, Backing vocals & lead guitar
  • Scott Shriner, Backing vocals & bass

SZNZ: Summer is an EP by Weezer, the second (of four) releases in the SZNZ series. Rivers Cuomo described the EP's sound as "21st century 90s". Aesthetically, he linked the project to 44 BC at the end of the Roman Republic (in contrast to the Celtic theming around SZNZ: Spring), with an intended emotion of "youthful indignation".

Side One

  • Lawn Chair
  • Records
  • Blue Like Jazz
  • The Opposite of Me

Side Two

  • What's the Good of Being Good
  • Cuomoville
  • Thank You and Good Night
  • SZNZ: Summer is the eighth EP by American rock band Weezer, as the second of four EPs in their SZNZ project. It was released digitally on June 21, 2022, coinciding with the Summer solstice. The EP received its lead single "Records" just a few days before its release.
  • On June 12, 2022, fans discovered short snippets of songs from the album in the source code to Cuomo's web browser game
  • SZNZ was originally spelled "SZNS"
  • The album’s cover art was leaked on June 10, 2022 via a listing on Amazon France.
